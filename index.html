Bit.ly Resolver - Chrome Extension

Were you even scared to click a bit.ly link? Not knowing where it might lead? That is only one reason to use the extension, other reasons can simply be the mere fact that a bit.ly link just doesn't give you any context of the page it takes you to. Looking at http://bit.ly/abc123 won't tell you anything. But looking at http://www.cool-cat-tshirts.com/ tells you all you need to know.

Enjoy
